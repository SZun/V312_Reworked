ABOUT US

At Vape312, we like to emphasize the idea of quality. Our extensive juice selection consists of the top e-liquid brands throughout the nation. Along with our free mod service, our hadware selection varies to fit any smoker, whether it's someone who vapes socially with friends, or someone who is trying to quit smoking, our staff will work with you to help build the perfect setup to suit your needs.

We embody the spirit of Chicago from our Lakeview location. Along with our quality product, our customer first mindset helped us win the award of "Best Antismoke Shop" by the Chicago Reader. Come visit us and see why all of Chicagoland prefers Vape312 for their smokeless needs.
